# ATLAS Wiki Schema

## Domain

Operational intelligence for L2 ATLAS: missions, sources, integrations, runtime decisions, product architecture, market research, and reusable workflows.

## Required files

- `index.md` — content catalog.
- `log.md` — append-only chronological activity.
- `raw/` — immutable source material.

## Page types

`entity`, `concept`, `decision`, `workflow`, `source-summary`, `comparison`, `query`, `risk`.

## Rule

Raw sources are immutable. Synthesis goes into wiki pages. Every new page must be indexed and logged.
