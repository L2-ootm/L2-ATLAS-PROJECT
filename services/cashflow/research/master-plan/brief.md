Produce a phased execution master plan for transforming L2 Cashflow from an internal Next.js+SQLite tool into a universal modular financial platform.

- Current state: Next.js 16 + React 19 + SQLite/Supabase + 17 tables, internal L2 tool

- 42 research findings files already produced (590+ sources)
