# F3: Competitor Module Architecture

## Findings

### [1] Xero uses a 3-tier progressive feature unlock model (Early/Growing/Established) with a core module set that expands by quantity limits and feature depth rather than adding entirely new modules
- quote: "Early: Send quotes and 20 invoices, 5 bills. Growing: Send invoices and quotes, Automate bill entry and track bills. Established: 180 day cash flow forecast, Analyze KPIs and ratios, Use multiple currencies, Track time and costs for projects"
- url: https://www.xero.com/us/pricing-plans/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [2] Xero's core module taxonomy is: Invoicing, Bills/Expenses, Bank Reconciliation, Reporting, Payroll, Inventory, Projects, Expenses — with the App Store adding 10+ extension categories
- quote: "Popular app categories: Payroll HR, Invoicing and jobs, Inventory, Ecommerce, CRM and marketing, Debtor management, Documents, Accountant tools, Bills and expenses, Time tracking, Point of sale"
- url: https://apps.xero.com/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [3] FreshBooks organizes features into 5 explicit category groups on its pricing page: Track & Organize Client Info, Billing Solutions, Client Payment Options, Managing Your Business, and Add-ons
- quote: "Track And Organize Client Info | Billing Solutions | Client Payment Options | Managing Your Business | Add-ons"
- url: https://www.freshbooks.com/pricing
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [4] FreshBooks tiers by billable client count (5/50/unlimited) as the primary gating mechanism, with advanced accounting (double-entry, bank reconciliation, accountant access) locked behind Plus tier
- quote: "Lite: 5 Clients. Plus: 50 Clients — Double-entry accounting reports, Bank reconciliation, Accountant access. Premium: Unlimited clients — Project profitability, Accounts Payable, Automatic bill receipt data capture"
- url: https://www.freshbooks.com/pricing
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [5] Wave uses a 2-tier freemium model (Starter $0 / Pro $19/mo) organized into 3 functional groups: "Stay organized & tax-ready" (Accounting, Receipts, Mobile app), "Get paid" (Invoicing, Estimates & Deposits, Payments), "Pay yourself & your team" (Payroll)
- quote: "Stay organized & tax-ready: Accounting, Receipts, Mobile app. Get paid: Invoicing, Estimates & Deposits, Payments. Pay yourself & your team: Payroll"
- url: https://www.waveapps.com/pricing
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [6] Wave gates automation features (auto-import bank transactions, auto-merge/categorize, automated late payment reminders, branded invoices) behind the Pro tier, while keeping core bookkeeping and invoicing free
- quote: "Starter: Create unlimited estimates, invoices, bills, and bookkeeping records. Pro: Auto-import bank transactions, Auto-merge and categorize bank transactions, Digitally capture unlimited receipts, Automate late payment reminders"
- url: https://www.waveapps.com/pricing
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [7] Nubank Business (Brazil) structures its product around 6 distinct financial modules: Conta (account), Cartão (card), Empréstimos (loans), Soluções para cobrança (billing/charging solutions), Investimentos (investments), and Central de Impostos MEI (MEI tax center)
- quote: "Conta | Cartão | Empréstimos | Soluções para cobrança | Investimentos | Central de Impostos MEI"
- url: https://nubank.com.br/empresas/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [8] Mercado Pago for Business separates into "Conta Digital" (digital account) and "Conta Negócio" (business account), with the business tier adding: Soluções de vendas (Maquininhas, Point Tap, Tap to Pay, Link de pagamento, Pix, Checkout, Assinaturas, Sistema de Gestão) and Serviços financeiros (Cartão, Empréstimos, Rendimentos)
- quote: "Conta Negócio: Soluções de vendas — Maquininhas point, Point Tap, Tap to Pay no iPhone, Link de pagamento, Pix, Checkout para loja virtual, Planos de assinatura, Sistema de Gestão. Serviços financeiros — Cartão de crédito, Empréstimos, Rendimentos e investimentos"
- url: https://www.mercadopago.com.br/empresas
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [9] PagBank/PagSeguro (Brazil) organizes business features into: Maquininhas (POS hardware with 3 pricing plans — Essencial, Super Max, and volume-based tiers), Venda Online (Checkout, Link de Pagamento, Pagamento Recorrente), Soluções Empresariais (Conciliação Financeira, PlugPag, TEF, PagTotem, Folha de Pagamento), and Conta PJ (free business account with Gestão de acessos, Gestão de Cobranças, Pagamento Múltiplo)
- quote: "Maquininhas: Essencial (no minimum), Super Max (R$2,000+/mo). Venda Online: Checkout, Link de Pagamento, Envio Fácil, Gestão de Cobrança. Soluções Empresariais: Conciliação Financeira, PlugPag, TEF, PagTotem, Folha de Pagamento"
- url: https://pagbank.com.br/para-seu-negocio
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [10] All platforms universally include Invoicing/Billing and Payments as core MVP modules; Payroll and Advanced Reporting are consistently gated behind higher tiers or sold as paid add-ons ($25-40/mo across FreshBooks, Wave, and Xero)
- quote: "FreshBooks Payroll: $40/mo plus $6/mo per user. Wave Payroll: from $25/mo. Xero: Payroll available on Growing and Established plans"
- url: multiple (freshbooks.com/pricing, waveapps.com/pricing, xero.com/us/pricing-plans/)
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [11] Brazilian fintech platforms (Nubank, Mercado Pago, PagBank) bundle banking services (account, card, investments, insurance, credit) alongside payment acceptance, whereas US/UK platforms (Xero, FreshBooks, Wave) treat banking integration as a connection/feature rather than a core product module
- quote: "Nubank: Conta + Cartão + Empréstimos + Investimentos. Mercado Pago: Conta Digital + Cartão + Crédito + Investimentos + Seguros. PagBank: Conta PJ + Cartões + Investimentos + Seguros"
- url: nubank.com.br/empresas, mercadopago.com.br/empresas, pagbank.com.br
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high

### [12] Xero's App Store marketplace organizes integrations by function (Payroll HR, Invoicing and jobs, Inventory, Ecommerce, CRM and marketing) and by industry, with the most popular categories being Ecommerce sync (A2X), approval workflows (ApprovalMax), and job management (ServiceM8) — indicating the marketplace fills gaps in inventory management, time tracking, and industry-specific workflows
- quote: "Most popular apps: Syft (Reports|Graphs|Forecasts), ApprovalMax (payment control), ServiceM8 (job management), A2X (ecommerce accounting). Categories: Payroll HR, Invoicing and jobs, Inventory, Ecommerce, CRM and marketing"
- url: https://apps.xero.com/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026
- confidence: high
