# F4: Compliance & Regulatory Requirements

## Findings

### [1] Brazil's SPED (Sistema Publico de Escrituracao Digital) mandates four core fiscal book modules: ECD (Escrituracao Contabil Digital), ECF (Escrituracao Contabil Fiscal), EFD-Contribuicoes, and EFD-ICMS IPI — each with dedicated validator/generator programs distributed by Receita Federal
- quote: "ECD Baixe aqui o programa validador da Escrituracao Contabil Digital (ECD). ECF Baixe aqui o programa validador da Escrituracao Contabil Fiscal (ECF). EFD-Contribuicoes Baixe aqui o programa gerador da Escrituracao Fiscal Digital das Contribuicoes incidentes sobre a Receita (EFD-Contribuicoes). EFD - ICMS IPI Baixe aqui o programa validador da Escrituracao Fiscal Digital de ICMS IPI."
- url: https://www.gov.br/receitafederal/pt-br/centrais-de-conteudo/download/sped
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [2] Simples Nacional is a unified tax regime for micro and small enterprises (MEI, ME, EPP) governed by Lei Complementar 123, consolidating federal, state, and municipal taxes into a single DAS payment — with distinct sub-regimes for MEI (SIMEI) and ME/EPP
- quote: "CGSN define prazos de opcao pelo Simples Nacional e pelo regime regular do IBS e da CBS para 2027"
- url: https://www8.receita.fazenda.gov.br/SimplesNacional/
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026-04-17
- confidence: high

### [3] MEI (Microempreendedor Individual) requires monthly DAS payment via PGMEI generator, annual DASN-SIMEI declaration, and has specific obligations including not fiscal emission, relatorio mensal, and cadastro atualization — distinct from ME/EPP Simples Nacional
- quote: "PGMEI - Programa Gerador do DAS para MEI. DASN SIMEI - Declaracao Anual para o MEI."
- url: https://www.gov.br/empresas-e-negocios/pt-br/empreendedor
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [4] Brazil's tax regimes include six distinct categories requiring different compliance: MEI (annual revenue limit ~R$81k), ME (up to R$360k), EPP (R$360k-R$4.8M), LTDA (limited liability company), SA (sociedade anonima), with Simples Nacional (MEI/ME/EPP), Lucro Presumido, and Lucro Real as the three main tax calculation methods
- quote: (Portal do Empreendedor services structure: MEI, ME, EPP as distinct tiers with separate DAS payment, DASN-SIMEI, and registration obligations)
- url: https://www.gov.br/empresas-e-negocios/pt-br/empreendedor
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [5] Brazil's electronic invoicing system requires three distinct document types: NFe (Nota Fiscal Eletronica for goods), NFS-e (Nota Fiscal de Servico Eletronica for services, governed by ABRASF/city-level rules), and CT-e (Conhecimento de Transporte Eletronico for freight) — each with separate SEFAZ authorization
- quote: "NFS-e MEI" link present on Simples Nacional portal; NFe and NFS-e portals are separate federal systems (nfe.fazenda.gov.br, nfse.gov.br)
- url: https://www8.receita.fazenda.gov.br/SimplesNacional/
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high

### [6] SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002) requires public company CEO/CFO to individually certify accuracy of financial information quarterly (Section 302), mandates internal controls assessment and audit (Section 404), and imposes criminal penalties for fraudulent financial activity — applies to all US public companies and affects any financial platform serving them
- quote: "The Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 is a United States federal law that mandates certain practices in financial record keeping and reporting for corporations... top management must individually certify the accuracy of financial information."
- url: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sarbanes%E2%80%93Oxley_Act
- source_type: secondary
- published: 2002-07-30 (enacted)
- confidence: high

### [7] SOX Section 404 requires management assessment of internal controls over financial reporting, and Section 302 requires quarterly CEO/CFO certification — creating demand for financial systems to provide audit trails, segregation of duties, access controls, and automated compliance reporting
- quote: "Section 302 requires that the company's 'principal officers' (typically the chief executive officer and chief financial officer) certify and approve the integrity of their company financial reports quarterly."
- url: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sarbanes%E2%80%93Oxley_Act
- source_type: secondary
- published: 2002-07-30
- confidence: high

### [8] IFRS Accounting Standards are required in over 140 jurisdictions globally, with IFRS 17 (Insurance Contracts) effective from 1 January 2023 requiring risk-adjusted present value measurement, contractual service margin recognition, and separate presentation of insurance revenue vs finance income
- quote: "IFRS Accounting Standards are, in effect, a global accounting language—companies in more than 140 jurisdictions are required to use them when reporting on their financial health."
- url: https://www.ifrs.org/content/ifrs/home/issued-standards/list-of-standards/ifrs-17-insurance-contracts.html
- source_type: primary
- published: 2023-01-01 (effective)
- confidence: high

### [9] Brazil is undergoing a tax reform replacing PIS/COFINS/ICMS/ISS/IPIL with a dual VAT model: CBS (Contribuicao sobre Bens e Servicos, federal) and IBS (Imposto sobre Bens e Servicos, subnational), with a pilot program already launched by Receita Federal — financial systems must prepare for this transition
- quote: "Aderir ao Piloto da Reforma Tributaria da Contribuicao sobre Bens e Servicos (CBS)" (listed under Reforma Tributaria services on gov.br)
- url: https://www.gov.br/receitafederal/pt-br/servicos/reforma-tributaria
- source_type: primary
- published: 2026 (pilot active)
- confidence: high

### [10] A universal financial platform must implement modular compliance engines covering: (a) tax regime-specific calculation (Simples/Presumido/Real per regime), (b) electronic document generation and SEFAZ integration (NFe/NFS-e/CT-e), (c) fiscal book production (SPED ECD/ECF/EFD), (d) tax calendar management with automated deadlines, (e) audit trail and access control for SOX/internal controls, and (f) multi-GAAP reporting (Brazilian GAAP + IFRS + US GAAP)
- quote: (derived from all sources: SPED programs page confirms 4 fiscal book modules; Simples Nacional portal confirms regime-specific services; SOX confirms internal control requirements; IFRS confirms multi-jurisdictional standards)
- url: multiple primary sources above
- source_type: primary
- published: unknown
- confidence: high
