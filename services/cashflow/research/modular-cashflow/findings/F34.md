## Finding 1: Brazilian Payroll Components — Complete Inventory

A CLT (Consolidação das Leis do Trabalho) payroll is composed of mandatory, statutory, and optional components. Every component has specific legal basis and calculation rules.

| Component | Legal Basis | Employer/Employee | Rate/Basis | Frequency |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|------------|-----------|
| **Salário** | CLT Art. 457 | — | Contractual amount (min. salário mínimo) | Monthly |
| **13º Salário** | Lei 4.749/1965 | Employer cost | 1/12 of salary per month worked (min 15 days) | 2 installments: Nov + Dec |
| **Férias** | CLT Art. 129-145 | Employer cost | 1/12 of salary per 12 months worked | After 12-month aquisitivo |
| **1/3 Constitucional** | CF Art. 7º, XVII | Employer cost | 33.33% sobre férias | Paid with férias |
| **FGTS** | Lei 8.036/1990 | Employer | 8% of remuneration (2% apprentice, 11.2% domestic) | Monthly deposit |
| **INSS (employee)** | Lei 8.212/1991 | Employee | Progressive: 7.5%-14% (2026 table) | Monthly withholding |
| **INSS (employer)** | Lei 8.212/1991 | Employer | 20% + RAT (1-3%) + terceiros (5.8%) | Monthly via DCTFWeb |
| **IRRF** | Lei 7.713/1988 | Employee | Progressive: 0%-27.5% | Monthly withholding |
| **Vale-Transporte** | Lei 7.418/1985 | Employee (max 6%) | Cost of commute; employee deduction capped at 6% of base salarial | Monthly |
| **Vale-Refeição/Alimentação** | PLR agreements | Employer (usually) | Varies by agreement; typically R$25-75/day | Monthly |

**Key insight**: The total employer cost ("encargos") for a single employee typically adds 68-80% on top of gross salary, depending on benefits structure.

## Finding 2: eSocial Event Architecture — S-1200/S-1210/S-1299 and DCTFWeb

eSocial is Brazil's unified digital bookkeeping system for labor, social security, and tax obligations. Current layout version: v. S-1.3 (as of NT 06/2026, production since 01/07/2026).

### Core Payroll Events

| Event | Code | Purpose | Deadline | Key Data |
|-------|------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Remuneração** | S-1200 | Report employee remuneration and withholdings | By 15th of month following payment | CPF, salary, FGTS base, INSS base, IRRF base, deductions |
| **Pagamentos** | S-1210 | Report actual payment data | By 15th of month following payment | Payment date, amount, method, chave pix |
| **Fechamento** | S-1299 | Close the payroll period | By 15th of month following payment | Period summary, total employees, total remuneration |
| **Admissão** | S-2200 | Register new hire | Day before admission date | Employment data, salary, benefits, CBO code |
| **Alteração Contratual** | S-2206 | Contract modification | By effective date | New salary, function, work conditions |
| **Desligamento** | S-2299 | Termination | By 10 days after termination | Termination date, cause, FGTS balance, 13º proportional |
| **FGTS** | S-1200 (embedded) | FGTS data is part of S-1200 | Same as S-1200 | Base de cálculo FGTS, alíquota, depósito |
| **Comunicação de Afastamento** | S-2230 | Leave/absence notification | By 16th of month following start | Leave type (INSS, maternity, etc.) |

### DCTFWeb Integration

DCTFWeb is the monthly digital tax declaration that replaces DIRF for employer contributions:
- **Integração automática**: eSocial events (S-1200/S-1299) generate DCTFWeb data automatically
- **INSS employer**: 20% + RAT + terceiros → calculated from S-1200 totals
- **Contribuição assistencial**: sindical contributions from S-1299 totals
- **Timeline**: DCTFWeb must be transmitted by 15th of month following payment; FGTS GPS by 20th

### Digital Certificate Requirements

- **eSocial requires** ICP-Brasil digital certificate (e-CPF or e-CNPJ) for XML signing
- All events are signed with XAdES-BES format using SHA-256
- As of NT 06/2026: new security certificate standard being implemented (production 24/06/2026)
- API integration available via REST (endpoint: `https://webservices.producaorestrita.eSocial.gov.br/`)

## Finding 3: FGTS Calculation — Alíquota, FGTS Digital, GPS

FGTS (Fundo de Garantia do Tempo de Serviço) is a mandatory employer deposit into Caixa Econômica Federal accounts.

### Rates

| Worker Category | FGTS Rate | Legal Basis |
|----------------|-----------|-------------|
| CLT employees (general) | **8%** | Lei 8.036/90 Art. 15 |
| Apprentices | **2%** | Lei 10.097/2000 Art. 15 |
| Domestic workers | **11.2%** | LC 150/2015 |
| Temporary workers (PJ) | **N/A** | Not applicable |
| Pro-labore (MEI/EPP) | **N/A** | Not subject to FGTS |

### Calculation Example (R$5.000 salary)

```
FGTS base: R$ 5.000,00
FGTS deposit: R$ 5.000,00 × 8% = R$ 400,00
```

For 13º salário and férias, FGTS is calculated on the proportional amount:
- 13º: FGTS on the 13th salary amount (1/12 per month worked)
- Férias: FGTS on (férias + 1/3 constitucional)
- Aviso prévio indenizado: FGTS on aviso prévio amount

### FGTS Digital

FGTS Digital replaced the GPS (Guia da Previdência Social) for FGTS deposits:
- Employer generates GRU (Guia de Recolhimento do FGTS e de Information à Previdência Social) via FGTS portal
- Payment via Pix or boleto bancário
- Deadline: 20th of month following payment month
- Integration with S-1200: FGTS data is included in the remuneration event

### FGTS Withdrawal Events

FGTS can be withdrawn by employee on:
- Dismissal without just cause (demissão sem justa causa)
- Retirement
- Purchase of own home
- Serious illness (cancer, HIV/AIDS)
- Account inactivity for 3+ years (annual withdrawal)

## Finding 4: INSS Calculation — Progressive Table 2026

INSS (Instituto Nacional do Seguro Social) is calculated progressively. Employee portion is withheld from salary; employer portion is 20% + RAT + terceiros.

### Employee INSS Table (2026 — based on current table with annual adjustment)

| Faixa | De (R$) | Até (R$) | Alíquota | Dedução (R$) |
|-------|---------|----------|----------|---------------|
| 1ª | 0,00 | 1.517,25 | 7,5% | 0,00 |
| 2ª | 1.517,26 | 2.793,88 | 9,0% | 22,76 |
| 3ª | 2.793,89 | 4.190,83 | 12,0% | 106,59 |
| 4ª | 4.190,84 | 8.157,41 | 14,0% | 190,40 |

**Teto INSS 2026**: R$ 990,45 (updated annually; the teto determines maximum contribution)

**Progressive calculation method** (since 2020 reform):
```
Example: Salary R$5.000,00

1ª faixa: R$1.517,25 × 7,5%  = R$  113,79
2ª faixa: R$1.276,63 × 9,0%  = R$  114,90  (2.793,88 - 1.517,25)
3ª faixa: R$1.396,95 × 12,0% = R$  167,63  (4.190,83 - 2.793,88)
4ª faixa: R$  809,17 × 14,0% = R$  113,28  (5.000,00 - 4.190,84)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Total INSS employee:              R$  509,61
```

### Employer INSS Components

| Component | Rate | Description |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| INSS patronal | **20%** | Base employer contribution |
| RAT (Risco Ambiental do Trabalho) | **1-3%** | Based on FAP and risk classification |
| Terceiros (S系统) | **5,8%** | Salário-educação (2,5%) + INCRA (0,2%) + SENAI/SESI/SEBRAE/etc. (3,1%) |
| Terceiros (substituídos) | **0,0%** | Companies with their own fund (e.g., Petrobrás) |
| **Total employer INSS** | **~26,8%** | Base (20%) + RAT (2%) + terceiros (5,8%) = 27,8% typical |

**For R$5.000 salary**: Employer INSS = R$5.000 × 27,8% = R$1.390,00 (additional employer cost)

## Finding 5: IRRF Calculation — Monthly Base de Cálculo

IRRF (Imposto de Renda Retido na Fonte) is calculated on the monthly base de cálculo.

### IRRF Table (2026 — based on current table)

| Base de Cálculo Mensal (R$) | Alíquota | Parcela a Deduzir (R$) |
|----------------------------|----------|------------------------|
| Até 2.259,20 | Isento | 0,00 |
| De 2.259,21 até 2.826,65 | 7,5% | 169,44 |
| De 2.826,66 até 3.751,05 | 15,0% | 381,44 |
| De 3.751,06 até 4.664,68 | 22,5% | 662,77 |
| Acima de 4.664,68 | 27,5% | 896,00 |

### Base de Cálculo Formula

```
Base de Cálculo = Remuneração Bruta - INSS Employee - Dependentes × R$189,59
```

- **Dependentes**: R$189,59 per dependent (spouse, children <21, students <24, parents 65+)
- **Pensão alimentícia judicial**: deducted at 100% (not limited)

### IRRF Calculation Example (R$5.000 salary, 1 dependent, no other deductions)

```
Remuneração bruta:              R$ 5.000,00
(-) INSS employee:              R$   509,61
(-) Dependente (1 × 189,59):    R$   189,59
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Base de cálculo:                R$ 4.300,80

IRRF = R$ 4.300,80 × 22,5% - R$ 662,77
IRRF = R$ 967,68 - R$ 662,77 = R$ 304,91
```

**Net salary**: R$5.000,00 - R$509,61 (INSS) - R$304,91 (IRRF) - R$300,00 (VT 6%) = **R$4.885,49**

## Finding 6: Payslip Generation — Mandatory Fields, Digital Delivery, Storage

### CLT Mandatory Fields (Holerite/Contracheque)

Per CLT Art. 464 and Lei 8.212/1991, the payslip must contain:

**Identification**: Employee name, CPF, NIT/PIS, CBO (occupation code), employer CNPJ
**Earnings**: Salário base, horas extras (with adicional legal), adicional noturno (20%), insalubridade/periculosidade, 13º proportional, férias proportional, DSR
**Deductions**: INSS employee, IRRF, vale-transporte, pensão alimentícia, sindicato (if opted)
**Employer charges**: FGTS, INSS patronal, RAT, terceiros (shown on payslip for transparency)
**Totals**: Total proventos (gross), total descontos (deductions), valor líquido (net)

### Digital Delivery

- **CTPS Digital**: Since 2019, employment records are digital (via app CTPS Digital)
- **eSocial**: Digital event transmission replaces physical document delivery
- **Holerite eletrônico**: Employer must provide digital payslip; employee access via portal or app
- **Mandatory digital delivery** since MP 905/2019 (Lei 13.874/2019)

### Storage Requirements

- **5 years** from termination of employment (CLT Art. 11, §1º)
- **20 years** for FGTS-related documents (Lei 8.036/90)
- Electronic storage authorized since MP 905/2019 (CLT Art. 12-A)
- Must maintain integrity and authenticity (digital signature recommended)
- **Recommendation**: Store XML files from eSocial for minimum 5 years post-termination

## Finding 7: Pro-Labore vs Salário — MEI/EPP/LTDA Differences

The distinction between salário (employment relationship) and pro-labore (self-employed/partner compensation) has significant social security and tax implications.

| Attribute | Salário (CLT) | Pro-Labore (Autônomo/Sócio) |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------------|
| **Legal basis** | CLT (employment contract) | Lei 9.249/1995 Art. 12; Lei 8.212/1991 Art. 22 |
| **INSS employee** | Progressive 7.5-14% (withheld by employer) | 11% (self-pay via DAS/boleto) for MEI; 11% for EPP/LTDA sócio on pro-labore |
| **INSS employer** | 20% + RAT + terceiros | **N/A** for MEI; **N/A** for EPP/LTDA on pro-labore |
| **FGTS** | **8% mandatory** | **Not applicable** |
| **IRRF** | Monthly withholding by employer | Monthly withholding by company (or annual declaration for MEI) |
| **13º/Férias** | **Mandatory** | **Not applicable** |
| **eSocial events** | S-1200/S-1210/S-1299 | **S-1000** (employer data); pro-labore reported in **S-1200** for LTDA sócio |
| **CLT protections** | Full CLT rights | **None** (no FGTS, no stabilization, no aviso prévio) |
| **Work permit** | CTPS digital registration | No CTPS required |

### MEI Specific Rules

- MEI (Microempreendedor Individual): max 1 employee (who gets CLT), owner receives "pró-labore"
- MEI owner does NOT pay INSS as employee — pays fixed monthly DAS (includes INSS simplificado)
- Pro-labore for MEI owner: optional declaration; social security credit requires voluntary INSS contribution
- **R$5.000,00 cap** on pro-labore that MEI can declare (for INSS credit purposes)

### LTDA Sócio Pro-Labore

- Sócio-gestor (managing partner): company must withhold 11% INSS + IRRF on pro-labore
- Pro-labore is mandatory when sócio performs management activities
- FGTS not required on pro-labore (sócio is not employee)
- Pro-labore minimum: cannot be below minimum wage unless sócio has other income source

## Finding 9: Labor Cost Modeling — Total Cost of Employee

### Cost Per Employee Formula

```
Total Employee Cost = Base Salary + Encargos Obligatorios + Benefits + Variable Compensation

Where:
  Encargos Obrigatorios = INSS patronal + RAT + Terceiros + FGTS + Provisão 13º + Provisão Férias + 1/3 + Aviso Prévio
  Benefits = VT + VR/VA + PLR + Plano de Saúde + Outros
```

### Cost Breakdown Table (R$5.000 salary)

| Cost Component | Amount (R$) | % of Salary |
|----------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Gross Salary** | 5.000,00 | 100,0% |
| **INSS patronal** (20%) | 1.000,00 | 20,0% |
| **RAT** (2%) | 100,00 | 2,0% |
| **Terceiros** (5,8%) | 290,00 | 5,8% |
| **FGTS** (8%) | 400,00 | 8,0% |
| **13º proporcional** (1/12) | 416,67 | 8,3% |
| **Férias proporcional** (1/12) | 416,67 | 8,3% |
| **1/3 constitucional** (1/12) | 138,89 | 2,8% |
| **Aviso prévio** (proporcional) | 125,00 | 2,5% |
| **VT** (6% employee, employer top-up) | 300,00 | 6,0% |
| **VR/VA** (employer) | 1.100,00 | 22,0% |
| **Plano de saúde** (employer) | 500,00 | 10,0% |
| **Total Employee Cost** | **R$ 9.787,23** | **195,7%** |

### Cost Per Department Aggregation

For department-level modeling, aggregate:
- Sum of all employee total costs
- Shared costs: payroll system fees, eSocial compliance costs, HR overhead
- Seasonal adjustments: férias coletivas, 13º payment concentration in Nov/Dec

**Key insight**: A department with 10 employees at R$5.000 base salary has a true monthly cost of approximately R$97.872 — nearly 2x the raw payroll sum.

## Finding 11: Salary Provisions — 13º, Férias, Aviso Prévio

Employers must maintain monthly provisions for future labor payments.

### Provision Calculation (Monthly)

| Provision | Formula | Monthly Accrual (R$5.000 salary) |
|-----------|---------|----------------------------------|
| 13º salário | Salário ÷ 12 | R$ 416,67 |
| Férias | Salário ÷ 12 | R$ 416,67 |
| 1/3 constitucional | (Salário ÷ 12) × 1/3 | R$ 138,89 |
| FGTS on provisions | (13º + Férias + 1/3) × 8% | R$ 78,89 |
| **Total monthly provision** | — | **R$ 1.051,12** |

### Aviso Prévio Provision

Aviso prévio is proportional to years of service (minimum 30 days, max 90 days):
```
Aviso prévio proporcional = 30 dias + 3 dias por ano de serviço (máx 90 dias)
Monthly provision: (30 + (anos × 3) / 360) × (salário/30)
```
