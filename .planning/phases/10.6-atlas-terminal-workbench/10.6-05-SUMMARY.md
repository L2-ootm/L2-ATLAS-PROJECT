## Decisions Made
- Function signature follows the committed test's actual call shape (`session_id`, `run_id`, `expected_*_version` kwargs, no `lock`/`owner_token` passed by the test) rather than the plan prose's `(conn, lock, session_id, *, owner_token, owner_pid=None)` — per plan instruction, "Committed Wave-0 test is ground truth over plan prose."
- `replay_contract` is called explicitly inside `resume_or_fail_closed` using the caller's `run_id` and `expected_*_version` args, in addition to `resume_session`'s own internal replay against the session's stored versions. This satisfies test 1's monkeypatch assertions (`order == ["replay", "resume"]`) and test 2's requirement that a real, unmocked drift on the caller-supplied `run_id` blocks the resume — necessary because the `surface_session` fixture's session row has no `run_id` bound to the test's seeded contract snapshot, so relying solely on `resume_session`'s internal replay (keyed off `session.run_id`) would not exercise the intended drift path.
