### Blocker-Fix Invariant (verification method item 5)

VERIFIED. `permission_broker.claim()` issues NO status-flip UPDATE. Its only direct UPDATE on tool_approvals (`:525-529`) writes the `decision` column only, gated `WHERE status='pending'` — it leaves status pending and cannot race the at-most-once guard. The pending→terminal transition is delegated to `tool_service.approve()/reject()`, the single atomic authority. The reconcile UPDATE (`:627-629`) is the separate fail-closed `executing`→`failed` restart sweep, never in the claim race. Confirmed by grep: zero `UPDATE tool_approvals SET status` statements in permission_broker.py.

### AUD-02 Read-Only Confirmation (verification method item 6)

VERIFIED. `list_outcomes:232-256` and `get_outcome:259-278` are pure SELECTs: no `lock` parameter, no INSERT/UPDATE, no call into claim/approve/reject. Pending rows are excluded (only executed|rejected|failed projected), so an observer cannot mistake the read for a claimable queue and gains no decision authority. Test: `test_outcome_projection_read_only` asserts status is unchanged before/after the read.
