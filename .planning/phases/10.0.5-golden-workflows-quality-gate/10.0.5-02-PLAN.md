## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| T-1005-04 | Tampering | repo_triage workspace reads | mitigate | routed through tool_service.invoke -> policy.check_workspace_boundary; no path can escape workspace_root |
| T-1005-05 | Information Disclosure | repo_triage artifact content | accept | artifact bodies are markdown summaries of the operator's own workspace, written back into the operator's own wiki — no cross-tenant boundary exists in this single-operator system |
| T-1005-06 | Denial of Service | research_brief on an empty wiki | mitigate | empty search_wiki results produce an honest "no matches" artifact instead of raising, so repeated demo runs never crash on a fresh wiki |
